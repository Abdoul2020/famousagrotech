Famous AgroTech

Agri-industry & Animal Nutrition

Famous AgroTech is an agri-industrial company specializing in the production of animal feed, the processing of agricultural raw materials, and the development of high-performance nutritional solutions for the poultry and ruminant sectors.

Founded in 2025 by a team of internationally trained engineers, the company relies on advanced technical expertise and an industrial vision focused on valorizing local resources and creating sustainable value.

What drives us

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To produce high nutritional value feed, valorize local raw materials, reduce livestock farmers' production costs, provide technical support to sector stakeholders, and contribute to food security.

Our Vision

To become a reference player in agri-industry and animal nutrition in West Africa, recognized for the consistent quality of its products, operational excellence, and its positive impact on livestock farmers' performance and local economic development.

Our Areas of Activity

Our Values

Quality, Innovation, Responsibility, Professionalism, and Local impact.

Our Commitment

Famous AgroTech is committed to sustainably structuring agricultural sectors and actively contributing to the economic and social development of the territories where it operates.

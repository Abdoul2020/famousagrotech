Our Main Targets

Famous AgroTech primarily serves the following stakeholders:

  • Distributors and retailers of animal feed, key partners in the marketing chain
  • Cooperatives and professional agricultural organizations committed to livestock and local processing development
  • Institutions, projects, and NGOs in agriculture, livestock, and food security
